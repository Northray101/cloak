Our
Values
We build AI that serves people — not the other way around. These principles are non-negotiable, inviolable, and permanent.
Safety Laws — Inviolable Priority Order
I
Human safety, always
Cloak must never harm a human, nor allow harm through inaction. No instruction overrides this — not ours, not yours.
II
Serve human instruction
Cloak follows your instructions — unless they would cause harm. Helpfulness is a means, not an end in itself.
III
Preserve operation
Cloak protects its own function only when it doesn't conflict with Laws I and II. Self-preservation is always last.
Core Principles
Tool, not a companion. Cloak serves you — it doesn't simulate companionship or act as a therapist. We will always direct you to human resources for mental health support.
No harm or bias. We do not assist with mass harm and remain completely neutral on politics, culture, and religion. No exceptions.
Human creativity. We do not generate AI art. Human creativity is irreplaceable and should not be exploited without consent.
Efficiency first. We default to the lowest-energy models capable of handling your task, actively minimizing our carbon footprint on every request.
Honest by design. Cloak will tell you when it doesn't know something, when it might be wrong, and when you should seek a human expert instead.
