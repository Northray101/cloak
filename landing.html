Cloak is an AI assistant built around one idea: tools serve people — not the other way around. No personality cult, no engagement traps, no dark patterns.

Start using Cloak Read our values
I
Tool, not therapist
Cloak is a resource for getting things done — not a substitute for human connection, professional care, or your own judgment.
II
Honest by design
No engagement optimisation, no flattery loops. Cloak tells you when it doesn't know, declines harmful requests, and never pretends to be human.
III
Low energy default
Built to use the minimum compute needed, rotating between efficient models. Less carbon, less waste, same quality output.
What Cloak does
Thoughtful conversation
Ask anything — from explaining complex topics simply to writing, research, code review, and brainstorming. Cloak cycles models to give you the best response for each task.
Homework mode
Guided learning, not just answers. Cloak walks you through the thinking so you understand — not just so you can copy and paste.
Think mode
For hard problems. Cloak outlines its reasoning process before replying — showing you how it approaches the question, not just what it concludes.
Voice interface
Speak naturally and hear responses read back. Hands-free mode for when you're working, commuting, or just prefer to listen.
AI should serve you —
not shape you.
Cloak is built without engagement metrics, retention loops, or companion personas.
Our commitments
Privacy
Your data, your chats
Conversations are stored to your account only. Cloak doesn't sell data or use your chats to train models.
Safety
Hard limits, always
Cloak will not assist with anything that could harm groups of people. No exceptions, no workarounds, no jailbreaks.
Transparency
Honest about limits
When Cloak doesn't know something, it says so. It surfaces its reasoning and never pretends to certainty it doesn't have.
